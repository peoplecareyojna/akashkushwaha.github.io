BIOGRAPHY OF AKASH KUSHWAHA

Founder/Chairman - People Group International

Akash Kushwaha, founder of People Group International, achieved great achievements in his young age. Today he has given destination to many of his dreams. People live their life in different ways. Some dream to live life and some live life to fulfill their dreams. But in the battle of life only those who stick to their decisions are successful. Such is the story of Akash Kushwaha, founder of ‘People Group International’. In today's era, Akash Kushwaha has become an inspiration for the youth due to his strong intentions and courage.

Akash was a playful and sharp minded person since childhood, but he was not interested in studies at all. Akash was born on June 3, 1997 in a simple family of Sardarpur village located in Morena district of Madhya Pradesh. With his birth, there was an atmosphere of happiness in the family. Akash grew up lovingly in his family since childhood.

Akash's father's name is Uday Singh and mother is Mrs. Sunita Kushwaha and sister is Ms. Versha Kushwaha. Being the only son, the family left no stone unturned in his education and upbringing. He did his early education from the government school in the village itself. In the year 2006, while studying in Akash Primary School, he succeeded in the entrance examination of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya. But the family members did not let them get away from their loved ones at such a young age due to pampering. Due to which Akash's dream of studying in Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya remained incomplete.

After this he completed his studies till 8th from his village itself. Akash was admitted in class 9 to continue his further studies. But Akash failed in the exam. However, after hard work, he took the 9th exam again and this time the hard work paid off. Akash enrolled in 10th class after passing 9th but the exam was still pending. Akash also failed in the 10th examination. After that took the supplementary examination and passed.

Not seeing interest in Akash's studies, the family members sent Akash to his maternal uncle's house. However, for Akash, the real struggle of life started from here and after staying for 3 years, Akash one day left his maternal uncle's house and went out in search of the destination of life. During this time, Akash had only 10 rupees in his pocket. Without any plan, he left for the unknown city. During this, Akash himself did not know where to go and what to do. But he did not give up and kept his intention strong.

Akash worked as a laborer for two days after leaving his maternal uncle, from which he earned 600 rupees and then reached Gujarat after leaving here. After reaching Gujarat, Akash himself did not know what to do, but did not let the intention waver. During this Akash Kushwaha also faced many difficulties. After going to Gujarat, Akash learned to make artificial jewelery and after cutting that jewelery started sending it from Gujarat to Indore. While in this work he also suffered a lot. But did not give up and continued flying.

After some time he started the business of silver with a friend and invested all his deposits in this business. Even during this time, their hard work did not pay off and Akash failed in this business as well. Because the destination was written somewhere else in the destiny. Akash fell in love with a girl when he was in Gujarat. But after some time he also left Akash's side in difficult times.

After running out of money, Akash returned home. After returning, Kushwaha worked with lawyers after knowing something. In the year 2015, Akash opened a computer center named after his father named 'Uday Computer Center' and from here he never looked back and climbed the ladder of success day by day.

Shortly after opening the computer center, he along with his friends engaged in social welfare and opened 'New Culture Public School' for the education of young children and changed the computer center into 'Uday Solution' company. Akash did not have to stop here, the destination was yet to come.

He handed over his company to the CEO and came to Delhi, the city of Dilwalas, to achieve his destination. After coming to Delhi, Akash Kushwaha started a non-profit organization. Whose name was named 'People Care Organization'. But in the year 2018, this organization was changed to “People Care International Organization”. Actually, this organization works to help the poor and needy globally. While working for this organization in Delhi, Akash saw the problems of the society which are often not visible to the politicians and this problem was to improve the standard of living of the poor.

Aakash through his organization provided the facility of a card people care card. Through which the needs of people like health care, access to education to the poor and insurance are met. Through this organization, he has been working on many projects (People Poor Helping Team, Na Dhoka Hoga Na Crime, People Employment Solution Scheme, People ki Kitchen, People Free Coaching Center, People Government Project etc.).

Akash completed his Diploma course in Pearl Farming in the year 2018 and started his business in the same field. Akash also provides free training to people in this field, so that the younger generation can promote business in the coming times. Akash Kushwaha's name comes at the top of the list of those who have achieved high status at a young age. Akash Kushwaha is the editor-in-chief of People News Channel and People News magazine as well as the director.

Apart from this, there is also the state secretary of All India ‘Yuva Kushwaha Samaj’ from Madhya Pradesh. He also partnered with many companies and in the last 2019 he started People Group International. This group includes People Care International Organization, People Builders Private Limited, People Security Private Limited, People Digitals Private Limited, People News Private Limited, People Finance Private Limited, People Inn Private Limited, People Matrimonial Private Limited, People Business Consultancy, Anjali Sharper Key, Anjali Frauding Solution, M/S Navyug Enterprises.

It is said that success comes from experience and experience always comes from bad experience. Today this line has proved to be true on Akash Kushwaha.
